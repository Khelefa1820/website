Other Places In Kokernag

Daksum

Daksum (Kashmiri: located in the Green Forests of Jammu and Kashmir at Anantnag-Semthan-Kishtwar road (NH-1B), is a natural scenic spot. However, it is still to be counted in the Tourist Places of JK Tourism Department with Sinthan top

Sintan top

The Sinthan pass connects Kashmir valley with Jammu Province through Kistwar. It is 12,500 feet (3,800 m) above sea level and is snow-capped for most of the year. It is located on the Anantnag-Kokernag-Kishtwar section of NH 244, and is 130 km south of Srinagar and 73 km from Anantnag and 48 km from Kokernag.

Margan top

Margan Top or Margan Pass is a mountain pass, connecting Warwan Valley with the main Kashmir Valley. It is located south of Anantnag district in the Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir.[1][2]
